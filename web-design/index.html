Our Web Development Process

Below is a little summary of how we do things. We like our customers to feel comfortable with the process when we are building their website and understand why we do it the way we do.

Client Workshop

The first step of our process is a meeting with you to discuss everything you want and formulate the base to start the project. At this point we will discuss writing, images, colours etc along with target audience and what you are trying to achieve through our services.

Wireframe

Once we feel we have a firm grasp of what you want us to build we will put together a wireframe for your website and present it to you so you can get a feel of how your website will be structured and give us some feedback.

Design

We will then put together the design for your site and present you with the finished mockup or prototype based on your pricing tier. This is the point where you can give feedback on the final design and we will make revisions based on your input.

Website Build

Once you have confirmed the final design the website will be built using HTML, CSS and Javascript. All functionality and animations are added and the website is tested on multiple devices and browsers to ensure compatibility and speed.

Go Live

Once the website build is complete and hosting & domain is established, and you have paid your balance your website will be made live and submitted to Google search engine and you will be given a video tutorial on how to use the content management system to make changes to your site. Your site is now live. From here you can either manage your site yourself (not recomended) or you can purchase a maintenance package to have us look after your site for you. We will manage everything from seo to analytics to page speed and conversion rate.
