Our Web Development Process

Below is a little summary of how we do things. We like our customers to feel comfortable with the process and understand why we do it the way we do.

Client Workshop

The first step of our process is a meeting with you to discuss everything you want and formulate the base to start the project. At this point we will discuss writing, images, colours etc

Wireframe

Net we will present you with a wireframe for your layout so you can give some feedback on the structure of your website.

Design

We will then put together the design for your site and present you with the finished mockup or prototype based on your pricing tier. This is the point where you can give feedback on the final design and we will make revisions based on your input.

Website Build

Once you have confirmed the final design the website will be built using HTML, CSS and Javascript. All functionality and animations are added and the website is tested on multiple devices and browsers.

Go Live

Once the website build is complete and hosting & domain is established, and you have paid your balance your website will be made live and submitted to Google search engine and you will be given a video tutorial on how to use the content management system to make changes to your site.

Transparent Pricing & a Free Quote

Our pricing is transparent, and is easily viewable on our website. No having to send an email and go back and fourth for days just to find out a rough estimate of the price.
